About Me

I hold a B.S.E. in Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan. I have an interest in embedded systems design and development with a focus in hardware design and hardware/software integration.

I am from Chelsea, MI, a small town on the outskirts of Ann Arbor. I began to learn programming skills in middle school, and this eventually developed into a broader interest in electronics.

At Michigan, I joined the Michigan Aeronautical Science Association, where I spent nearly two years as the Avionics Director for the Clementine and Limelight projects. I led the design and creation of PCB design projects, implemented an industrial controls and data acquisition system, and managed application software projects.
